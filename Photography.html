Photography Subjects

Subject One: Tourism Through Florence, Italy

For this project I was tasked with choosing a topic related to the history of Florence and to capture it through a current lens. For my topic, I chose the crippling rise of tourism within the city and the effect it has on the people. I tried to capture this through photos of the most common businesses that arise as tourism grows and the effect that these places have on tourists. I added a black and white effect to sharpen the idea and to really emphasize the rich history that this city holds.
